## 1. Purpose

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") sets out the data-protection terms that apply
where ChainX processes personal data in connection with the Service. It does not
grant software-license rights or modify intellectual-property terms. [LEGAL REVIEW
REQUIRED]

---

## 2. Relationship to Main Agreement

This DPA supplements and is subordinate to the applicable main agreement. In the
event of conflict on data-protection matters, Section 26 governs. [LEGAL REVIEW
REQUIRED]

---

## 3. Definitions

| Term                           | Meaning                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Personal Data                  | Information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, as defined under [DATA PROTECTION LAW].                                 |
| Processing                     | Any operation performed on Personal Data, as defined under [DATA PROTECTION LAW].                                                             |
| Controller                     | The party that determines the purposes and means of Processing.                                                                               |
| Processor                      | The party that Processes Personal Data on behalf of the Controller.                                                                           |
| Data Subject                   | The identified or identifiable natural person to whom Personal Data relates.                                                                  |
| Subprocessor                   | A third party engaged to Process Personal Data.                                                                                               |
| Security Incident              | A breach of security leading to accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure of, or access to Personal Data. |
| Applicable Data Protection Law | [DATA PROTECTION LAW] and any other applicable data-protection legislation.                                                                   |

## 5. Processing Instructions

Where ChainX acts as Processor, it Processes Personal Data only on documented
instructions from the Customer, unless required otherwise by Applicable Data
Protection Law. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 6. Nature and Purpose of Processing

The nature and purpose of Processing are described in **Annex 1**. [LEGAL REVIEW
REQUIRED]

---

## 7. Categories of Personal Data

Categories are set out in **Annex 1** using structured placeholders only. No real
personal data is included in this template. Examples may include identifiers,
contact details, and account metadata — to be confirmed per use case.

---

## 8. Categories of Data Subjects

Categories of Data Subjects are set out in **Annex 1** (e.g., Customer's authorized
users, Customer's end investors — placeholders only). [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 9. Duration of Processing

The duration of Processing is set out in **Annex 1** and is generally tied to the
Subscription Term and applicable legal obligations. Specific durations are not fixed
here. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 10. Confidentiality

ChainX ensures that personnel authorized to Process Personal Data are bound by
appropriate confidentiality obligations. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 11. Security Measures

ChainX maintains appropriate technical and organizational measures, described by
category in **Annex 2**, which may include:

- Access control
- Encryption where applicable
- Secrets management
- Logging and monitoring
- Backup
- Incident response
- Least-privilege access
- Environment separation

This template does not disclose infrastructure secrets. No measure guarantees
absolute security. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 12. Subprocessors

ChainX may engage Subprocessors. Categories may include cloud hosting, database
provider, blockchain infrastructure, wallet infrastructure, KYC provider, payment
provider, AI provider, and email provider. Not every provider is used in every
deployment. Approved Subprocessors are listed in **Annex 3** (placeholders). [LEGAL
REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 13. Subprocessor Changes

ChainX provides a mechanism for notifying changes to Subprocessors and for the
Customer to object, as defined per agreement. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 14. International Data Transfers

Cross-border transfer mechanisms are to be defined per use case and law. This
template does not invent standard contractual clause obligations or transfer
mechanisms. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 15. Data Subject Requests

Where ChainX acts as Processor, it assists the Customer, to the extent reasonably
possible, in responding to Data Subject requests. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 16. Assistance to Customer

ChainX provides reasonable assistance to the Customer with its data-protection
obligations, taking into account the nature of Processing and information available.
[LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 17. Security Incidents / Personal Data Breach

ChainX notifies the Customer of a Security Incident affecting Personal Data without
undue delay after becoming aware, within **[NOTIFICATION PERIOD — LEGAL REVIEW
REQUIRED]**. This template does not invent legally binding hour limits. Incident
handling cross-references `../DISASTER_RECOVERY.md`. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 18. DPIA / Regulatory Assistance

ChainX provides reasonable assistance with data-protection impact assessments and
prior consultations where applicable. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 19. Audits

Audit rights, scope, frequency, and conditions are to be defined per agreement.
[LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 20. Records

Each party maintains records of Processing as required by Applicable Data Protection
Law. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 21. Data Return and Deletion

On termination or expiry, ChainX returns and/or deletes Personal Data as instructed,
subject to the agreement and Applicable Data Protection Law. Retention periods are
not fixed here and must be defined per agreement and law. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 22. Government Requests

Handling of government or law-enforcement access requests is to be defined, subject
to applicable law. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 23. Compliance with Applicable Law

Each party complies with its obligations under Applicable Data Protection Law. This
DPA does not guarantee any party's overall regulatory compliance. [LEGAL REVIEW
REQUIRED]

---

## 24. Liability

Liability under this DPA is governed by and subject to the limitations in the main
agreement. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 25. Termination

This DPA remains in effect for as long as ChainX Processes Personal Data in
connection with the Service, and terminates in accordance with the main agreement.
[LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]

---

## 26. Conflict with Main Agreement

In the event of conflict between this DPA and the main agreement regarding
data-protection matters, this DPA governs to the extent of the conflict, subject to
counsel's final determination of precedence. [LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]
